How To Set Up Your New Computer!!!

Part Two: Downloading Applications

Good job on completing the first step of setting up your computer! Unless you didn't connect your computer to the Internet. In that case, use this link to learn how to set it up!

Did you set up your Internet? Yes? Good! So now you have a computer which is connected to the Internet. Your computer will come with a few preinstlalled applications, one of which will be a search engine, probably either Internet Exploer or Safari, depending on your computer. You will probably want other applications, or maybe a different search engine, such as Google Chrome. Luckily, its easy to download applications from the Internet. In this example, I will be showing you how to download Google Chrome off the Internet. You can also download other applications and softwares such as anti viruses and games.

  1. Go to the preinstalled search engine and type in the search bar 'download google chrome'.
  2. Click the first link that shows up, entitled 'Download Google Chrome'
  3. You should arrive on the official Google Chrome download page. Click the blue button in the middle to download Chrome.

Now you have your Google Chrome downloaded, you can now download any other app from the Internet onto your computer. Just be careful of viruses. Finally, I will teach you how to share information on you computer. Just click this link!
